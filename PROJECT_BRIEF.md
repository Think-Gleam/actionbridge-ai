# ActionBridge AI — Project Brief

## Hackathon
AISeekho 2026 Google Antigravity Hackathon

## Selected Challenge
Challenge 1: Autonomous Content-to-Action Agent (Insight → Action System)

## Project Name
ActionBridge AI

## Core Concept
ActionBridge AI turns messy reports, complaints, news, and public updates into insights, recommended actions, simulated execution, and visible outcomes.

## Main Demo Scenario
- **Input:** "Orders in Lahore dropped by 25% this month. Delivery complaints increased, and customer satisfaction is falling."
- **Expected Output:** - **Insight:** Lahore faces both a demand decline and delivery experience issues.
  - **Impact:** Critical revenue and user retention risk.
  - **Recommended Action:** Launch a Lahore recovery campaign and dispatch the operations team to review regional delivery complaints.
  - **Simulated Execution:** Scripted background engine simulates building a targeted promotional event and auto-assigning a high-priority task ticket.
